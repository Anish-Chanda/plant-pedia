Plant Name: Monstera (Monstera deliciosa)

Zone: Suitable for zones 10-12; commonly grown as a houseplant in other zones.

Fact: Monstera, also known as the Swiss Cheese Plant, is a popular tropical plant known for its unique and fenestrated leaves.

Soil Type: Well-draining potting mix with organic matter; prefers slightly moist soil.

Flowering: Monstera plants may produce flowers, but they are typically inconspicuous. The plant is more renowned for its large, split leaves that develop as it matures.

Plant Name: Aglaonema

Zone: Generally grown as a houseplant; not cold-tolerant and thrives in warm indoor conditions.

Fact: Aglaonema, also known as Chinese Evergreen, is a popular and low-maintenance indoor plant valued for its attractive foliage and air-purifying qualities.

Soil Type: Well-draining potting mix with organic matter; prefers slightly moist soil but should not be waterlogged.

Flowering: Aglaonema can produce small, inconspicuous flowers, but it is primarily grown for its colorful and variegated leaves, which come in various patterns and shades.

Plant Name: Snake Plant (Sansevieria)

Zone: Adaptable to a wide range of zones; tolerant of different climates.

Fact: Snake plants, also known as Mother-in-Law's Tongue, are popular indoor plants valued for their hardiness and air-purifying qualities.

Soil Type: Well-draining potting mix; can tolerate various soil conditions but prefers slightly dry conditions.

Flowering: Snake plants produce tall spikes with small, fragrant, greenish-white flowers, although they may not bloom frequently when grown indoors as houseplants.

Plant Name: Succulents

Zone: Depending on the variety, succulents can thrive in various zones; often grown as houseplants in colder climates.

Fact: Succulents are a diverse group of plants known for their ability to store water in their leaves, stems, or roots, making them well-adapted to arid conditions.

Soil Type: Well-draining soil mix, specifically designed for succulents and cacti; good drainage is crucial to prevent root rot.

Flowering: Many succulents produce colorful and often showy flowers. However, their unique and fleshy foliage is a primary attraction.

Plant Name: Pothos (Epipremnum aureum)

Zone: Suitable for a wide range of zones; adaptable to different climates.

Fact: Pothos is a popular and easy-to-care-for houseplant known for its trailing vines and heart-shaped leaves.

Soil Type: Well-draining potting mix; can thrive in various soil conditions.

Flowering: Pothos plants are grown for their attractive foliage and are not primarily known for their flowers. However, they may produce small, inconspicuous blooms in the right conditions.

Plant Name: Fig

Zone: Suitable for warm, Mediterranean-like climates

Fact: Fig trees are known for their sweet and nutritious fruit called figs.

Soil Type: Well-draining soil, preferably sandy loam or loamy soil.

Flowering: Fig trees produce small, insignificant flowers that are actually inside the fruit, not externally visible.
